Sport season tips
Pick your sport to see what to focus on during that season.
🦵
Lateral quickness
Shuffle drills and ladder work. Quick feet win games.
⬆️
Jump & land safely
Practice soft landings — bend knees, absorb with hips. Biggest injury preventer.
🦶
Ankle mobility daily
Ankle circles and calf stretches after every practice.
😴
Cut volume on game weeks
2 workout days max. Legs need to be fresh to play your best.
🌀
Rotational core
Twisting core exercises give you more bat speed and throw power.
💪
Shoulder health
Wall slides and band pulls — do these on throwing days especially.
🚫
Skip push-ups before you throw
Don't tire your shoulder before a throwing session. Do them after.
🤸
Hip mobility every day
Hip flexor stretch + doorway chest stretch. Your arm will thank you.
🦵
Single-leg strength
Single-leg squats and balances. Runners need one strong leg at a time.
🍑
Glute strength = speed
Glute bridges and calf raises make you faster and stop shin splints.
🧘
Stretch every single day
Hip flexors and calves. Non-negotiable during XC season.
💤
Skip extra cardio
You're already running a lot. Extra runs = tired legs = slower races.
Training rules 📋
Follow these and you'll keep getting better — and stay healthy.
🚀
Warm up every time
5 min of jogging, arm circles, leg swings. Never skip it.
🏋️
No heavy weights yet
Bodyweight is plenty. Perfect form matters way more than heavy weight.
😄
Keep it fun
If it stops being fun, tell someone. Workouts shouldn't feel like punishment.
🌙
Sleep 9–11 hours
Sleep is your secret weapon. Your muscles grow while you sleep.
💧
Drink water
Before, during, and after. Especially during XC in the heat.
📈
Get a little better each week
One extra rep. One second faster. Small improvements add up huge.
⚠️
Tell an adult about pain
Knee pain below the kneecap or heel pain — stop and tell Mom or Dad right away.
